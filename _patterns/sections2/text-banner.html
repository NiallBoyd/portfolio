Typography

The Meadowlark Tutorials brand design logo uses Adonis Bold Italic. Adonis Bold Italic was cho- sen for its readability in the letter A.This was chosen specifically for those who have reading challenges such as Dyslexia. For print typeface and paragraphs Adonis Regular was chosen for body copy as well headings if needed. Bold is suggested for emphasis as well as text that needs to stand out for contrast.
